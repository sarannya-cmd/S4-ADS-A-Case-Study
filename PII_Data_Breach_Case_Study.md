## 2. Introduction
Organizations collect large volumes of personal data to provide digital services. This data includes Personally Identifiable Information (PII) such as names, addresses, phone numbers, financial information, and identification numbers. Exposure of such information can lead to identity theft, financial fraud, and privacy violations.

---

## 3. Background of the Organization / Case
Equifax is one of the largest credit reporting agencies in the world. The company stores financial and personal information of millions of individuals. In 2017, Equifax experienced a major cybersecurity breach when attackers exploited a vulnerability in the company's web application.

---

## 4. Problem or Violation Description
The breach occurred because a known software vulnerability was not patched in time. Attackers gained unauthorized access to Equifax systems and stole sensitive information including:

- Names  
- Social Security numbers  
- Birth dates  
- Addresses  

Approximately **147 million individuals** were affected.

---

## 5. Analysis (Laws / Principles Violated)

**Privacy Violation**  
Customer data was not adequately protected.

**Lack of Security Measures**  
Proper cybersecurity controls were not implemented.

**Failure to Protect PII**  
Sensitive personal information was exposed to unauthorized access.

**Lack of Accountability**  
Poor security governance allowed the breach to occur.

---

## 6. Impact / Consequences

### Impact on Individuals
- Identity theft
- Financial fraud
- Loss of privacy

### Impact on Organization
- Reputation damage
- Legal penalties
- Loss of public trust

---

## 7. Actions Taken or Penalties
Equifax agreed to a settlement of up to **$700 million** with regulators and affected individuals. The company also strengthened cybersecurity practices and monitoring systems.

---

## 8. Recommendations / Preventive Measures
- Implement strong cybersecurity systems
- Regularly update and patch software vulnerabilities
- Encrypt sensitive personal data
- Conduct security audits
- Use anonymization techniques when analyzing data

---

## 9. Conclusion
The Equifax data breach highlights the importance of protecting personal data. Organizations must implement strong privacy and security measures to prevent misuse of personal information.
